Skills:

  1. Passive Skills
    • Will To Recover: Able to regenerate from any kind of damage done to his body with the exception of fatal wounds, such as being stabbed through the heart.     • Longevity: Is immune to all diseases, poisons, and any abilities that can be harmful to his status. His regeneration speed is also far faster than a normal human's and increases exponentially when he sleeps.
    • Detoxification: Body is able to automatically cleanse itself of any poisons once they are detected in his bloodstream. Unfortunately, alcohol is considered to be a poison and as a result, Able can't get drunk.

    • Advanced Dagger Techniques: If uses his daggers in battle, any damage he inflicts on his opponent will be increased by 33%.
  2. Active Skills
    • Stealth: Able to camouflage with his surroundings and hide all traces of his presence, essentially turning himself both physically and magically invisible.
    • Bloodlust: Able to magically intimidate an enemy combatant into submission, inducing intense fear into those who are targeted by this technique and decreasing their stats by 50%. It can also be used on multiple opponents at once.
    • Quicksilver: Able to increase his speed by 30% for a short period of time.
    • Mutilation: Aims for a target's vital points before slashing away at them with his daggers, inflicting critical damage.
    • Dagger Rush: Barrages a target with his daggers from all directions.
    • Ruler's Authority: Able to move and control objects via telekinesis.
    • Dragon's Fear: Able releases a mana-infused shout from his soul, driving anyone weaker than him into a state of intense despair and panic.
  3. Job Skills
    • Shadow Extraction: Able to extract shadows from the corpses of his enemies and add them into his army.
    • Shadow Preservation: Able to preserve his shadows in storage and monitor them by perceiving their senses.
    • Shadow Exchange: Able to use shadows as portals to travel vast distances in an instant.
    • Monarch's Domain: Able to increase the strength of all of his active shadows by 50% in battle.
